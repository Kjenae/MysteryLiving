About Mystery Living

Hi, I'm Mackenzie!

I'm the heart behind Mystery Living. As a teacher, a believer, and a community cheerleader, I started this brand to blend faith, fun, and fashion. Each piece we launch is created with love to empower women to live boldly and joyfully.
